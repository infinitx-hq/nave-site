Grid texture: 28px graph-paper grid at rgba(31,224,122,.055) over the background. Radial emerald glows, never full-screen linear gradients (banding).

## Typography

- Display serif: "Newsreader", Georgia, serif — weight 400, tight leading (.97), italic for the green emphasis words.
- Chrome/labels: "DM Mono", ui-monospace, monospace — 11-24px, letterspaced .12-.2em, uppercase.
- Body/UI: "Hanken Grotesk", sans-serif.

## Motion personality

Confident builder energy: expo/power3 entrances, back.out punches on numbers, drawn rules (scaleX), mono lines that type/slide. Medium-high energy. No bounce-castle elastics on type except the hero "1".

## What NOT to do

- No word "template" anywhere.
- No pure #000 frames, no dead-gray neutrals.
- No fake UI of platforms (no Instagram/TikTok logos in the mosaic — abstract content pieces only).
- Claude Code is referenced by name + spark glyph; do not imply Anthropic endorsement.
- Honesty: "scheduled automatically" must carry "you approve, it ships" nearby.
