002 — The process

Three steps. No mystery.

01

Scoping call

We map your formats, channels, machines, and team. Then I quote a fixed price inside the $2,500–$7,500 range, before you pay a dollar.

02

The build

I build your Content OS in Claude Code and test every format on your real footage. The engine already works today, so it's weeks, not months — your handoff date is locked on the scoping call.

03

Handoff + training

I install it on your machines, train your team live, and we produce your first month together — roughly 30 finished pieces — so the system is proven while I'm still there.

Custom builds run $2,500–$7,500 one-time, priced on scope: formats, channels, machines, team size. A single freelance editor is $1,500+ a month. That's $18,000+ a year, every year, and you still own nothing. This you build once, and it's yours.
