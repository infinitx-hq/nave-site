    FAQ
    
Asked straight.

    

      
What is Claude Code — and do I need to be technical?
Claude Code is Anthropic's AI agent that runs on your own computer. You direct it in plain English — nobody here writes code. If you can install an app and paste a command, you're qualified. The install course walks every step; same-day support catches the rest.

      
Is this another course I won't finish?
No. The OS works the day you paste it in — the training is depth, not a prerequisite. The live calls and the 30-day guarantee exist for the version of you that's bought courses before.

      
Why not just hire an editor or an agency?
That's $1,500–5,000/mo, forever, for someone else's judgment of your brand. Here you own the operation and your voice is built into the system.

      
Can I run it for clients or multiple brands?
Yes — the OS installs per brand, each with its own voice locked in. Several of the pipelines inside were built running client work.

      
What if I cancel?
Cancel anytime from your account. The OS lives on your machine, so you keep everything you've built. What ends is the weekly drops, the calls, the support, and the room.
