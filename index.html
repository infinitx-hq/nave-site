Real questions. Straight answers.

    

      
01Do I need a separate AI or API key?
No. Nave runs on your own Claude Code — your existing Claude subscription, no separate Anthropic key. You bring keys only for the two tools it uses: AssemblyAI (transcription) and Zernio (publishing + DM automation). You pay those directly — no markup.

      
02What does "a month of content in an hour" mean?
You install once — that's the live session. After that it's about an hour of your time per month: the crew produces the content, your hour is the review. You check the staged queue and say "schedule it." Nothing publishes without your approval. (Runs on your Claude Code — Claude Max recommended for full month-runs.)

      
03Is the install really 1:1?
Yes — one hour, live, just you and me, on your machine. We install, place your keys, verify a real render, set up your brand, and kick off your first month before we hang up. After: the weekly community call + personal Loom support — your screen, your problem, a custom walkthrough within 2 business days.

      
04What do I actually own?
The whole content operating system, on your machine — every skill, agent, and workflow that makes your content. Yours to keep, adjust, and grow with. No subscription, no login to someone else's software, no middleman who can reprice you or shut you off.

      
05What's the guarantee?
The $397 install: Nave runs on your machine within 30 days, or we make it run, or you get your money back — written terms. The $89 self-serve: bounded setup support instead (no cash refund — it's a complete system you download, so once it's yours it can't be returned). Refund policy.

      
06Who should NOT buy this?
You, if: you don't film and won't start (Nave needs your footage — you drive, it supports) · you want 100% hands-off (nothing posts without your review) · or you want it built and tuned FOR your business without touching it — that's the custom build.
