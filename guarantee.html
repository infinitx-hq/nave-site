The promise, in one line

Nave runs on your machine, or we make it run. If we can't, you get your money back.

Who this covers

This guarantee applies to buyers of the Founding Build Cohort ($397). The $89 Template carries a different promise — bounded install support, not a cash refund. See the section near the end.

The window

30 days from your cohort date (the date of the live workshop session you attend, or your first scheduled session if you miss it). [30 days = suggested default — confirm.] Claims must be opened by email within this window.

What "runs" means

Nave counts as running on your machine when all three are true:

  • The install script (install.ps1 / install.sh) completes and its verification step passes (the test render reports "render verified"), and
  • Your first content piece renders end to end from your own footage, and
  • This happens with the tool versions current as of your cohort date.

Scope: this is a get-it-running guarantee. It is not a warranty that upstream vendors (Anthropic, AssemblyAI, Zernio, or others) will never change their APIs, pricing, or terms after your window closes. It is also not a guarantee of views, followers, or revenue.

What we'll do to make it run

If Nave isn't running on your machine:

  • Email us at [support email] with a short description and, if possible, the error output.
  • We respond within 2 business days.
  • We attempt the fix with you: up to three (3) scheduled live screen-share sessions and unlimited async Loom walkthroughs within the window. [Session count = draft — confirm.]

Your part

  • Your machine meets the stated minimums (Windows / macOS with Homebrew / Debian-based Linux; the installer handles Node 20+, Python 3.12, ffmpeg, yt-dlp, jq).
  • You give us a reasonable shot at fixing it: share the error output, attend or schedule at least one screen-share or watch the Loom we send, and try the documented fix.
  • You hold an active Claude subscription (Nave runs inside your own Claude Code).

If your machine turns out to be unable to meet the minimums and we can't find a workaround, that still counts as "we couldn't make it run" — you get the refund.

The refund

If, after the steps above, Nave still does not run on your machine within the window:

  • You receive a full refund of $397 to your original payment method.
  • Processed within 10 business days of the refund decision.
  • No exit interviews, no hoops.

What this guarantee does not cover

  • Outcomes beyond "running": views, growth, revenue, or any business result.
  • Upstream vendor changes (API, pricing, terms) occurring after your window closes.
  • Refund requests opened after the window, except at our discretion.
  • Cases where the buyer declines to attempt any fix with us. (We have to be allowed to try — that's the "we make it run" half of the promise.)
